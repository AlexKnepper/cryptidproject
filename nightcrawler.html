Fresno Nightcrawlers

Description

An oddity alone in how recently they’ve been sighted, the Fresno Nightcrawlers have gained a large amount of interest in social media circles as well as crypid enthusiasts themselves. The Fresno Nightcrawler resembles a white, cloth-like being similar to the lower half of a human being. The size of these creatures seems to vary anywhere from 5 to 7 feet tall.

History/Origin

These creatures have been spotted on multiple security cameras relatively recently, around Fresno, California. The first and most notable sighting was by a Fresno homeowner who caught it on security camera after being woken up by his dogs barking. Since then, much media has covered them, including syfy’s “Fact or Faked" series, who marked them as unconfirmable.
