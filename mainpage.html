Where Am I?

Welcome to the woods.

Here you will find a collection of the awesome and macabre phenomena known as cryptids; unexplainable beings that live amongst the trees and dark places of the world.

What are cryptids?

A cryptid is a legendary creature with origins anywhere from urban legend to folklore. From aliens to Bigfoot, any creature with shaky validity and a good story behind it can be classified as a cryptid.

Aren't these hoaxes?

Some of these creatures over the years have been proven to be fakes or frauds, while others have shown themselves to be completely normal things or events that people of the time did not understand. And yet others have shown no logical conclusion but no evidence of being false.

Why should I care?

In the same way we strive to save animals from extinction, it is our duty to see these creatures of mystery and lore do not fall into complete obscurity. Woe to those who forget what they fear as they walk through the night.

Where do I go from here?

Move on through the woods with care friends, there are many wonderous things to be seen, but do so with haste, for in the same way you hunt for these creatures, know you too, are hunted.
