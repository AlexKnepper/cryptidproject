The Mothman

Description

The Mothman is a 7 foot humanoid creature with a 10 foot wingspan. It’s most notable and universally reported feature is that of two large, glowing red eyes. As its name suggests, the creature most closely resembles a gigantic moth, and many artistic rendetions of given descriptions give it a humanoid form, although seeming to lack a traditional head.

History/Origin

The Mothman has primarily been sighted in and around Point Pleasant, West Virginia. Mothman’s first sighting was on November 12th, 1966 by five men digging a grave, then three days later by a couple during a night time drive. Over 100 people came forward during the following year to give their own accounts. Some speculate The Mothman is a herald of calamity.
