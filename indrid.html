Indrid Cold

Description

Indrid Cold, also known as “the smiling man”, is most often described as a mostly normal human man standing at a height of around six feet and appearing to be in his mid 30s. He is usually described as having a fair complexion, dark hair, and wearing a large coat. The most striking feature of this being, and his namesake, is his large, unnerving smile that seemingly never breaks.

History/Origin

Indrid Cold was most often and famously discovered in West Virginia. The first instance of his sighting was recounted by Woodrow Derenberger, a salesman returning home from work, on November 2nd, 1966. Mr. Derenberger was stopped by a “cigar shaped craft” from which Indrid Cold exited and spoke to him telepathically. Mr. Derenberger claimed to be visited many times.
